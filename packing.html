Bulk Packing (In Bales)

Our most popular option for high-volume importers. We use industrial hydraulic presses to compress wipers into dense bales. This method maximizes container load capacity, significantly reducing shipping costs per kilogram. Bales are wrapped in heavy-duty woven polypropylene and secured with high-tensile strapping.

  • Sizes Available: 45kg, 55kg, 100kg, 400kg.
  • Protection: Waterproof HDPE outer layer.
  • Efficiency: Maximizes 40HC container weight capacity.
  • Customization: Transparent or opaque wrapping options.
Request Quote for Bales

Palletized Packing

Ideal for distribution centers with forklift capabilities. Goods are stacked in standard cartons or compressed blocks onto ISPM-15 compliant wooden or plastic pallets. The entire load is shrink-wrapped and corner-guarded to ensure stability during ocean transit and inland trucking.

  • Standards: Euro Pallets or Standard Industrial Pallets.
  • Security: Heavy-gauge shrink wrap & corner posts.
  • Handling: 4-way entry for easy forklift access.
  • Labeling: Barcoded pallet manifests included.
Request Pallet Rates

Retail Packing in Bags

Designed for direct-to-consumer sales or shelf display. We offer small-format packaging ranging from 1kg to 10kg in clear poly bags or branded packaging. We can apply your custom header cards, barcodes (UPC/EAN), and logos, making the product ready for retail sale immediately upon arrival.

  • Sizes: 1kg, 2kg, 5kg, 10kg handy bags.
  • Branding: Custom printed header cards & labels.
  • Outer Pack: Retail bags packed into master cartons.
  • Material: High-clarity LDPE or biodegradable options.
