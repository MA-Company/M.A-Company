Our Story

Founded in the industrial heart of Karachi, Pakistan, M.A. & COMPANY began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality textile solutions that meet the rigorous demands of international markets. Over the last two decades, we have evolved from a local supplier into a global powerhouse in the textile recycling and manufacturing sector.

We specialize in processing industrial wiping rags, institutional linens, and workwear. Our state-of-the-art facility ensures that every piece of fabric that leaves our warehouse meets strict quality control standards. By bridging the gap between raw textile availability and finished industrial needs, we serve clients across the USA, Europe, and the Middle East.
